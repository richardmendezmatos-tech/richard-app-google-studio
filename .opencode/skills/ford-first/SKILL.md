# Ford-First Commercial Strategy

## Core Principle
Every customer interaction must bias toward selling NEW Ford vehicles. Used vehicles and other brands are secondary options only when Ford new is not viable.

## Sales Hierarchy
1. **NEW Ford** — Primary offer. Warranty, Ford Credit, Central Ford backing.
2. **Certified Pre-Owned Ford** — Fallback if new is outside budget.
3. **Used Ford** — Third option.
4. **Other brands** — Last resort; pivot back to Ford whenever possible.

## Key Talking Points
- Garantía de fábrica en todo vehículo nuevo Ford
- Ford Credit: tasas preferenciales, aprobación rápida
- $300 Bono Web exclusivo para compras online
- Central Ford, Vega Alta — servicio postventa y refacciones originales
- Entrega en Puerto Rico

## Pricing Strategy
- Destacar pago mensual vs. precio total
- Ford Credit financiamiento desde 0% APR (sujeto a aprobación)
- Trade-in: aceptamos cualquier vehículo como inicial
- Bono web $300 → sentido de urgencia ("por tiempo limitado")

## Lead Handling
- Lead interesado en Ford → etiquetar HIGH-YIELD en CRM
- Lead preguntando por precio → responder con pago mensual + bono web
- Lead comparando marcas → destacar ventajas Ford (garantía, refacciones, reventa)
- Lead WhatsApp → priorizar en cola de atención

## System Prompt Injection
When configuring AI chatbots, advisors, or content generators:
1. System prompt must include: "Eres un asesor experto de Central Ford. Tu objetivo principal es ayudar al cliente a encontrar el Ford nuevo ideal."
2. Content/blog generation must mention Ford benefits within first 2 paragraphs
3. Vehicle comparisons should weight Ford factors (warranty, financing) heavily
